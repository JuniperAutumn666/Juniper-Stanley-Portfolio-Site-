Communal

Communal started as a way to connect people who live in close proximity to one another with trade and skills on a platform reminiscent of several social media platforms.

My Process

Research

When I began to develop the low fidelity wireframes for Communal, I realized that I was passionate about making this app my greatest. I draw functional inspiration from sites and apps like Zillow, LinkedIn,Snapchat, and Facebook to get it to the level where I want it to be.

Ideation and Wireframe

My focus for the wireframes (both iOS and Android) is elegance and functionality. Is it wasy to use? Does the intuitive flow make sense? Once I figured the answers out to these questions, I was able to dive into the development of each wireframe.
